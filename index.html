Setup tiers

Tier 1 · premium
3-tap reversal at historical level. Level respected across multiple sessions or with weekly/monthly history. 3rd tap is your highest-conviction entry. Wait weeks for these — they run 10-20R.
Tier 2 · standard
2nd tap reversal. Asia or pre-session formed the 1st tap. London or NY hour 2-3 retest with 5M rejection wick = your entry. Bread and butter. Expect 1-2 per week.
Tier 3 · break/retest
1H candle body closes through standard level. Wait for 15-20 pip push beyond, then drop to 5M for the retest rejection holding the broken level. No compounding allowed on Tier 3.

Risk and management

Risk1% account on Trade 1. Reinvest 3R on Trade 2 if Tier 1 or 2.
SLBeyond zone edge or standard level + 5-8 pip buffer. Structure dictates.
TPNext standard level giving 3R+ minimum. If not available, no trade.
1R hitSL to breakeven immediately.
2R hitSL to +1R. Close 50% at 3R.
RunnerSL at entry. Aim for next 4H or weekly level.
Manual exitIf price stalls 2-3 candles before TP, exit. Don't wait for SL.

Hard no-trade rules

  • No standard level inside zone
  • Inside tight consolidation
  • First hour of London or NY
  • Outside active windows
  • Target less than 3R to next level
  • Cloud chopping inside
  • 1st tap of fresh zone against trend
  • Major news within 2 hours
  • Friday afternoon (gap risk)
  • Tier 3 without 1H body close confirmation

Cloud sync settings

Sync your trades to Google Sheets so you can access them from any device.

How to set up sync

  1. Read the SETUP.md file in the repo
  2. Create a Google Sheet, deploy the Apps Script
  3. Paste your deployment URL and secret key above
  4. Click "Test connection" then "Save"

Without sync, data is stored locally in this browser only.
